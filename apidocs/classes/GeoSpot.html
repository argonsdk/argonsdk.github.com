GeoSpot Class

Module: ARGON

A GeoSpot represents a known, fixed location. GeoSpots can be used to obtain tighter registration by overriding the device GPS when a user indicates that they are at the precise location. GeoSpots can also be used for for "remote" AR experiences by leveraging panoramic imagery taken from the location.
